crab (作者应该不维护了 9年没更新)

crab目前仅提供协同过滤算法。常见的有以下功能模块

  • MatrixPreferenceDataModel implementation stores and provides access to all the user and item data as also the associated preferences needed in the computation.
  • UserSimilarity defines the notion of how similar two users are;It is based on one of many possible metrics or pairwise distance calculations.
  • NearestNeighborhood implementation defines the notion of a group of users that are most similar to a given user. At least, the UserBasedRecommender implementation pulls all these components together in order to recommend items to users, and related features.
